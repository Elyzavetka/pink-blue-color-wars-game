Color In-Line

Game Instructions

In this game, two players can take part. Each player has their color (blue or pink). Players take turns putting their colors in empty squares. The first player to get 4 cells of their color in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner. If all 16 squares are full and no player collects 4 cells of their color in a line, the game ends in a tie.
